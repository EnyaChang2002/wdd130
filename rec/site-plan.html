Content

Home page

Recommendation part: State the fiction name, summary and reason for recommending. The fiction name will be bold, the reason is very important as the audience needs to know why the fic is so good to read. There also should be spoiler alert at the part where is will have spoilers, using bright bold red letters. Readers review, warnings (that may or may not contain spoilers) (that stating the fic have triggering element), and tags. The website of the fiction, the name of the author. I want the audience to have an easier time finding fictions that are interesting and share my favorite fiction to them, at the same time share my opinion. The site range from free website like Articles of Our Own (Ao3) to pay-to-read website (晉江). There will be good comments and neutral comments. I want the child page to have child page to make the page cleaner and the spoilers and reviews that contain spoilers have a place to stay. The language: Considering that the English and Chinese part of the rec won’t be the same, I will only separate them by placing them in the different part of the same page. Side bar: The side bar contain the newest update of recommended fictions and information about the site owner. The email will be used as a means of contact. Navigation: The stories are sorted from A to Z in English and from the number of strokes from less to more in Chinese. The top will have the new fictions name but the audience will have to scroll to the right place to see the content, or, if I make grandchildren page it will be open to click in the article. Thoughts: I’ve read many fanfictions on jj and Ao3, spent a lot of time searching. On the matter of reading fanfictions I believe there’s lots of people easily triggered as me and wish to have a more thorough warning so I may add another reading review website in the future. I want to have a place to store my recommendation list and want to see more people recommending fictions, most often I encounter problem where the reader doesn’t give a reason for recommend the fiction. And as the audience I don’t feel interested (if so I will click in after reading the summary, the reason for recommending if seeing more people’s opinion). There’s also this problem with how much spoiler count as spoiling. How should I state the reason and other problems. Other than that, I also considered writing a list of not recommended fictions but thought about if this is too extreme, and the pay-to-read part, well, I paid and it’s not like I directly leave bad reviews on the book but on the free-to-read fiction there may be problem where people would thought I was being a picky eater? But also that’s just my personal opinion so those who said don’t like don’t read could just, not read it. The reason I want to have this fiction recommendation started, other than wanting better recs is words said on some of the platform need to be very careful and could be banned but if I place it on my own website I could say whatever I want. Besides the possible ban for word usage, there’s also the way to avoid confronting the fan of the fiction and the author, avoid leaving bad reviews on the author and hiding my reading account. And, the problem of whether I should start a new email for this, because my original email is already busy enough as it is.
